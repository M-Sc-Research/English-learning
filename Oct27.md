✅ تاپیک امشب پارت ۲ اسپیکینگ آیلتس

🗣️ Describe a change you would like to make in your daily routine.

You should say :

🔹what the change is
🔹why you want to make it
🔹how it would affect your day
🔹and explain how easy or difficult it would be to do.
